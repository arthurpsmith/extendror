ROR Wikidata Extension

This is a demonstration of a proposed extension of the ROR research organization identifiers, to link institutions with their sub-units in any hierarchy, whether or not the particular sub-unit has its own ROR identifier. This particular implementation uses Wikidata item identifiers as the extension identifiers, so the full id looks something like 05rrcem69/Q101018345.
